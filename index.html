Second Chance

The two boys Chrom and Ren were best friends since they were a children. The two grew older and decided to become polices. Then during a dangerous mission, Chrom and Ren were both greatly injuries and while escaping a bullet was shot towards Chrom, but Ren steps in front of Chrom blocking the shot. Backup came, but it was too late, Ren died.

Chrom quits his job the next day due to the pain and the feeling that it was all his fault. Chrom believes in resurrection, so he did research on that for a whole year, until he found a book that records a mysterious event where time travel happened. It was said to be caused by an hourglass located inside a Maya temple.

Chrom sets out on a adventure to find the hourglass and travel back in time to prevent the death of his best friend
